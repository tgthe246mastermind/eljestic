Why I do what I do?

Our Story

My journey to Eljestic Home Care Services began with a leap of faith, leaving my home country to start a new life in Barbados. It was a difficult transition, filled with loneliness and moments of deep sadness as I grappled with homesickness and depression. I missed my family and the life I had known, and it felt like a part of me was missing.

But a new chapter opened when I began working with the elderly. In their stories, their resilience, and their gentle wisdom, I found a new sense of purpose. My heart began to heal, and my perspective on life shifted. It was through them that I discovered the incredible joy and fulfillment that comes from serving others.

One person, in particular, will forever hold a special place in my heart: Mrs. Velveteen Greenidge. She wasn’t just a client; she was a beacon of light in my life. Her warmth, her grace, and her unwavering spirit taught me more about life and love than I ever thought possible. She inspired me to see that every day is a gift and that the simple act of caring for another human being is a profound privilege. It was her memory that became the foundation for Eljestic Home Care Services.

Today, my goal is to ensure that every individual we serve feels the same positive impact that Mrs. Greenidge had on me. We're not just here to provide care; we're here to build connections, share laughter, and enrich lives.

Our dedicated team at Eljestic is committed to delivering the highest quality of care. We believe in treating every client with the dignity, respect, and compassion they deserve. We are here to be a source of comfort, support, and friendship, ensuring that you or your loved one can live with joy and independence in the comfort of your home.

We look forward to welcoming you into the Eljestic family.
